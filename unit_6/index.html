Self Care

Check your pulse—Have you had a moment to breathe today?

Just because there is no "I" in motherhood doesn't mean you shouldn't dedicate time to focusing on yours truly.

Sure, between your partner's happy hour and/or shuffleboard habit, your kiddo's swim, soccer, and interpretive dance classes, and your dog’s vigorous walk schedule, it can be hard to pencil in a few moments of rest and relaxation for good ol’ mom. But can you even attempt to be the best parent you are capable of being without checking in on your own psyche? Here we make the case for clearing the schedule for a serious detox of distractions and responsibilities. A few minutes of working on yourself can do wonders for your family.
